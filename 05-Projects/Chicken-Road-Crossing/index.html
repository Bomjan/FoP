Chicken Road Crossing

How to Play

Objective: Help the chicken cross the road safely and reach the top!
Controls:
  • ↑ Arrow Up - Move up
  • ↓ Arrow Down - Move down
  • ← Arrow Left - Move left
  • → Arrow Right - Move right
Scoring: Reach the top to earn 50 points and advance to the next level!
Lives: You have 3 lives. Avoid the cars or lose a life!
Difficulty: Cars get faster as you level up. Good luck!
